Moves and node types

Castle Node

Your king commands your army, keep him safe or you lose. He is always placed on a blue square, and always on the edge of the map.

Army Camp

Your Army Camps are the backbone of your army, keep them well connected and well supplied, so they can expand your territory. Army camps that have their supply lines cut will fall.

Fort

A fortified Army Camp, Forts can deploy Sappers.

Sapper

A temporarily deployed unit which can decrease terrain elevation by one level, and will take two turns to return to its Fort. Sappers can indirectly cut paths and attack Army Camps and Forts if they can reduce their elevation to be more than one level below adjacent paths.
